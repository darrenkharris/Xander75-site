Privacy

Last updated: June 2025

This is a static personal website for Xander75, a DJ persona. It does not use a database, does not have a server backend, and does not collect personal information from visitors.

This page is provided for transparency. It is not legal advice.

What information is collected?

This website does not actively collect personal data. There is no contact form, no mailing list sign-up and no user accounts on this site.

If you contact me directly by email (via the address listed on the site), any information you provide will be used solely to respond to your message. It will not be shared with third parties.

Hosting and server logs

This site is hosted on a third-party platform (Cloudflare Pages or similar). That platform may collect standard server log data, such as IP addresses and browser types, as part of its normal operation. This is governed by the host's own privacy policy, not mine.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not set its own cookies and does not include analytics or tracking software by default.

External platforms

This website links to external platforms where mixes, social content and bookings live. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Mixcloud — mixcloud.com/privacy
  • Instagram — Instagram Privacy Policy
  • SoundCloud — soundcloud.com/pages/privacy

When you click through to any of these platforms, their terms apply.

Questions

If you have questions about this privacy information, get in touch via the Connect section.
