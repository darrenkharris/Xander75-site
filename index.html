The Sound

House is home. Everything else is an upgrade in tempo.

House music is at the core — everything from deep and spiritual through to driving tech house and the harder end of the spectrum. It's not about sticking to one sub-genre; it's about reading the room and picking the right groove for the moment.

When the energy calls for it, the BPM climbs and the set shifts into Drum & Bass or UK Garage territory — providing something different without losing the thread. That flexibility is what makes a set interesting: the journey matters as much as any single track.

All mixes are recorded and available on Mixcloud. No set ever sounds quite the same twice.

Hear the Mixes
House Music 125–132 BPM

Deep, soulful, afro, tech, hard — all sub-genres welcome. House is the heartbeat. Whether it's rolling late-night grooves or peak-time bangers, this is where it starts.

Drum & Bass 170–174 BPM

Liquid, neurofunk, rollers. DnB gets deployed when the floor needs a gear change — it's the curveball that resets the energy and opens new doors in a set.

UK Garage 130–138 BPM

Classic and contemporary UK Garage — the shuffle, the swing, the bass pressure. Bridges the gap between House and DnB in a way nothing else quite manages.
